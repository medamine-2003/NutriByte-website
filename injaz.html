About Us

Meet the Team & Motivation

We are first-year students in Telecommunications Engineering at the National Engineering School of Tunis (ENIT). This project was developed as part of the INJAZ El Arab student competition, which empowers youth entrepreneurship and innovation.

Our mission was to design a smart, affordable solution to simplify pet care. NutriByte leverages AI and IoT to automate feeding and monitor pet behavior remotely.
